MHRC Decisions

The Ministry Human Resource Committee (MHRC) meetings are held every Monday from 2:15 PM onwards. The MHRC committee are the Department heads from the four departments under MoIC and HR members.


The MHRC decisions can be viewed from the calendar where the MHRC events are marked in color.
